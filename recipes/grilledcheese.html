Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 2 American Cheese Slices
  • Butter or Vegetable Oil Spread

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat up butter or spread until melted on a medium heat.
  2. Insert two pieces of bread, flipping to get both sides buttered.
  3. Toast one side, and then flip. Apply a piece of cheese to the toasted side.
  4. When cheese is melted, apply the cheese-side of the bread together and remove from heat.
  5. Serve by itself or with tomato soup.
